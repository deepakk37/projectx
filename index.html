Why take this course?

Deep Learning & Machine learning techniques are transforming the way industries are collecting, analysing and inferring data across all sectors. We at Project X Labs are excited about the potential of AI and want to right skill students aspiring to work on the most challenging problems and build a career in AI.

We start out by building your basics in Python and Math and then transition into advanced techniques of Deep Learning (specifically NLP and Computer Vision) and Machine Learning. The entire curriculum is 'project based' which means we learn, code and implement models on the go.

CourseFeatures

Project Based Curriculum

Our curriculum strives for a good balance between theory and practical. We focus a lot on relevant Math and Python concepts to build strong basics. For the AI related topics we begin with theory but quickly pivot to python notebooks and assignments to start implementing them.

Cutting Edge Content

We have gone an extra mile and taken inputs from some of the top researchers in the field of AI. The content topics, its theory, python notebook and projects have been sourced from and curated with inputs from top academicians in this field.

Research Paper Implementation

We believe that the best way to think about problems is to refer to work which has already tried to solve it. Thus the focus will be on using research paper to get a day 1 answer for thr problem at hand and then build upon it. 1 research paper each for NLP & Computer Vision will be implemented.

Live Projects

The projects in our curriculum are actual live problems that someone is grappling with and needs some of the best brains to work on. We will solve it live with you and come up with an answer.A total of 3 liveprojects have been included in the curriculum to solve real life problems.
